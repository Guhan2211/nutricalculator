Nutrition Calculator

This app analyzes your recipie with the ingridients you provide and replies you with the nutrional facts about your recipie.

Usage instructions:

  1. Enter your ingridients one by one with comma separation.
  2. You must give quantity as 1kg chicken or 2spoon sugar likeways.If you didn't mention the quantity it will assume for single unit.
  3. Click submit button
  4. Your recipie's nutrional data will be shown in table with some facts about it.
  5. If you want to edit your recipie you can just click and change it in the textarea.
